Is Your Sponsored Post ASCI-Compliant?

The exact disclosure rules every Indian creator needs to know in 2026.

Quick Answer

What ASCI Requires

Label any paid content clearly with "Advertisement," "Sponsored," or "Paid Partnership with [Brand]" — placed upfront, in the first two lines or on-screen, never buried in hashtags or behind "read more."

Which Tags Count

Accepted vs Rejected

Compliant:

#Ad#SponsoredPaid Partnership

Not compliant:

#collab#spthanks brand

People Also Ask

Quick FAQs

Does a free product need disclosure too?

Yes — any material connection, paid or gifted, needs a clear label.

Who gets penalized — me or the brand?

Both. CCPA can act against the influencer and the brand together.
