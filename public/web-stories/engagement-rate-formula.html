What's Your Real Engagement Rate?

The exact formula brands use to judge your profile — and how you stack up.

The Formula

How to Calculate It

(Likes + Comments) ÷ Followers × 100

Add total likes and comments on a post, divide by your follower count, multiply by 100. That's your engagement rate as a percentage.

Why It Matters

Followers Get Attention.
Engagement Gets Deals.

A 50K creator at 5% engagement reaches more real people per post than a 500K creator at 1%. Brands increasingly check this number before follower count.

Watch Out For

The Fake-Follower Trap

Bought followers tank your engagement rate instantly — and brands notice. A smaller, real audience always out-converts a padded one.

People Also Ask

Quick FAQs

Why do small accounts have higher rates?

Smaller audiences feel more personal — a bigger share of them actually likes and comments.

Should I include saves and shares?

For a quick check, likes + comments is enough. For a fuller picture, add saves and shares too.
