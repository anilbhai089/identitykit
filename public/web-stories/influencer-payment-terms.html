When Do You Actually Get Paid?

The 3 payment structures every creator should know before signing.

Quick Answer

The Industry Standard

Most brand deals use a 50% deposit at signing, 50% within 30 days of going live. Small deals are often 100% upfront; large campaigns split across milestones.
